Open in mobile SoundCloud App

Directly open a SoundCloud track in the mobile app,
via redirection to aspecial URL.

go

Redirect to a special URL (soundcloud://tracks:{id}).
http://soundcloud-open-in-app.herokuapp.com/go/{soundcloud_url}
